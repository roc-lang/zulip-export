Topic: --output flag

Richard Feldman (Oct 19 2023 at 18:35):

I'm thinking of adding an --output CLI flag with these characteristics:

  • you can pass --output path/to/foo.exe (or foo.o or foo.dylib or foo, whatever you like)
  • it will be used to determine where the output binary ends up
  • the default is current_dir.join(app_filename.with_extension("")).join(default_extension) (where default_extension depends on the other flags, and might be ".exe", "", or ".o")
