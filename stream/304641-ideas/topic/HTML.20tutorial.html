Brian Hicks (Nov 09 2022 at 15:12):

color in dark mode can be tough. Some things I've used:

  • for most text, flip HSL values. I like to use the HSLuv color space for this, as it doesn't have muddy transitions and maps better to how you perceive colors
  • reduce saturation, since colors "pop" differently on dark backgrounds.

In this case, I'd probably just desaturate+lighten the purple and teal. They'll still be in the same color family.
