In response to this topic.

For apps, instead of:

app [main] packages [Stdout from "https://…"]

main =
    Stdout.line "Hello, World!"

I would prefer:

app
    exposes [main]
    imports [Stdout from "https://…"]

main =
    Stdout.line "Hello, World!"
