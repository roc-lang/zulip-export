Sky Rose (Dec 12 2024 at 01:04):

+1 that multiple scrollbars is probably easier to navigate even with the potential to scroll the wrong half.

Some other ideas to avoid having two separate scrollable areas:

  1. Denser list of values in the side bar, with less whitespace, so it's more likely to fit on one screen without having to scroll at all.
  2. More organized side bar, grouping the exports into sections that can be expanded or collapsed. Again so that the content is shorter and might not have to scroll.
  3. A minimap like in many IDEs. Obviously much more complex to design, but couples the position in the sidebar/minimap to the main content so they scroll together.
