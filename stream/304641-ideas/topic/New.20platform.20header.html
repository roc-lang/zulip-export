Anton (Jul 04 2023 at 10:44):

In response to this topic.

Instead of:

platform package [Stdout, Stdin]
    requires [main]
    provides [mainForHost] to "prebuilt-hosts/"
    packages [
        Foo, Bar, Baz from "https://…",
        Something as Smt from "https://…",
    ]

I would prefer:

platform-package
    exposes [Stdout, Stdin]
    requires [main]
    provides [mainForHost] to "prebuilt-hosts/"
    imports [
        Foo, Bar, Baz from "https://…",
        Something as Smt from "https://…",
    ]
