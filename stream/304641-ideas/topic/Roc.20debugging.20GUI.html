Topic: Roc debugging GUI

Anton (Jul 12 2024 at 17:48):

For a while I've been thinking about a GUI app meant to make it easier to both debug and understand the compiler and the binaries it produces.

I want it to:

  • make our existing debugging techniques more discoverable
  • reduce friction in debugging
  • display debug output (e.g. from prints activated using the env vars in .cargo/config.toml) in a nicely structured way
  • be a place where custom tools (like checkmate and Joshua's debug_buf_viewer) can be integrated and maintained
  • encourage the development of debugging/visualisation tools
  • make (parts of) the compiler easier to understand for newcomers

Flamegraphs have helped us a lot and I think there's a lot of untapped potential for other graphical/interactive tools.
