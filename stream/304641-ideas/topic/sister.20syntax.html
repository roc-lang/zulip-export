Richard Feldman (Feb 22 2023 at 16:20):

historically I've seen a couple of things come out of alternate syntaxes:

  • The alternate syntax is popular among some but not all people using the language, and it fractures the community - Reason is an example of this
  • The alternate syntax sees very little use in practice - every project I can think of which fits the description "this does not change the semantics of the language but uses a syntax based around S-Expressions" falls into this category :sweat_smile:

Richard Feldman (Feb 22 2023 at 16:21):

so based on how such things have worked out historically, I think if we went out of our way to support this, we should expect negative results in practice

Richard Feldman (Feb 22 2023 at 16:21):

...which is why the plan is not to do that! :big_smile:
