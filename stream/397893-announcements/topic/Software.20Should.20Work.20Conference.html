Announcing Software Should Work, an independent conference on software reliability on July 16-17 in Columbia, MO.

Software is usually buggy and slow, but it doesn't have to be. That's why I’m organizing a conference on the tools and techniques we use to make it correct and fast instead.

If you're excited about Roc, there's a very good chance you'll enjoy this conference too.
@Richard Feldman, @Ray Myers, @John Murray, @Andrew Kelley , and Hillel Wayne are all speaking. I'd love to have a strong contingent of Roc fans at SSW and really we already do. :smiley:

A huge thank you to RWX (@Dan Manges @Tommy Graves) and our other sponsors for helping make the conference possible.

Tickets are available now https://softwareshould.work
