@everyone

October is just around the corner, which means it’s time to prepare for Roctoberfest :tada: 

Hacktoberfest

Registration for https://hacktoberfest.com/ open in a couple of days. I’ve added the “hacktoberfest” label to roc-lang/roc to let others know roc is open to contributions from the open-source community.

If anyone is not sure where to start, we have our Good First Issues.

Remember to submit four PR’s during October to qualify for the digital badge.

Contributor Mentoring

If you are interested in mentoring, please add your availability to this Mentors When2Meet. 

Contributors (both new and existing) can use this to find a good time with you and arrange to pair on an issue or ask for help.

Also, please add “Roctoberfest Mentor” in your zulip status, so people know you are open to help others. 

Contributor Fireside Chats

I’d like to arrange a number of fireside chats during Roctoberfest – just some informal meetings (not recorded or anything) between contributors to talk about anything related to Roc. 

If you are available for a fireside chat, please fill out your availability in this Fireside When2Meet. I’ll look for good times where people are available to chat across various time zones, and make a follow up post with the dates.

Roctoberfest Streaming

A few people have expressed interest in streaming again for Roctoberfest this year. Can you please let me know what you’re planning?

I would love to share your stream so we don’t miss it. 

Looking forward to hacking with you all for another Roctoberfest :rock_on:
