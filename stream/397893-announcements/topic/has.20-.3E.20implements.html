@everyone breaking changes are coming with tomorrow's nightly release!
The ... | ... has ... syntax will become ... where ... implements ...

For example:

-empty : {} -> Dict k v | k has Hash & Eq
+empty : {} -> Dict k v where k implements Hash & Eq
