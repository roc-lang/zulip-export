Richard Feldman (Sep 01 2023 at 14:58):

for those who are new here since last year, Roctoberfest is where we get a group of people together to go through last year's Advent of Code in Roc, during the month of October. This lets us find out how the new user experience is right now, what papercuts or bugs or missing pieces there are for a nice Advent of Code experience, etc...while still having the month of November to make improvements before the actual Advent of Code!

Richard Feldman (Sep 01 2023 at 14:59):

this was really helpful last year, and it surfaced a lot of things that otherwise we wouldn't have known to prioritize for last year's Advent of Code

Richard Feldman (Sep 01 2023 at 14:59):

so if anyone knows people who might be interested in participating this year, it's never too soon to start asking around!
