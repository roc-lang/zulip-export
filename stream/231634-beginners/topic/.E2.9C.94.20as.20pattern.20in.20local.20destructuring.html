In that longer example, I see no need for as because it's safer to do those two acts in two steps
Act 1: naming known pieces
Act 2: naming unknown pieces

Act 1 (safe):

triple : (Foo, Bar, Baz)

(first, second, third) = triple

Act 2 (unsafe):

Test a = first

Act 2 (safe):

when first is
    Test a -> ...
    Toast a -> ...
    _ -> ...
