Alex Nuttall (Mar 07 2024 at 14:02):

It can be used for pattern matching

startsWithS = \str ->
    when Str.toUtf8 str is
        ['s', ..] -> Bool.true
        _ -> Bool.false

Richard Feldman (Mar 07 2024 at 16:48):

Richard Feldman (Mar 07 2024 at 16:49):
