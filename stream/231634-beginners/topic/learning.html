Oskar Hahn (Feb 17 2024 at 14:33):

Roc does not use linked list but arrays/vectors. So you don't need a recursion.

last = \lst ->
    when lst is
        [] -> Nothing
        [.., x] -> Just x

See: https://www.roc-lang.org/tutorial#pattern-matching-on-lists
